Chlorophyll, Populaiton and GDP

Chlorophyll: Chlorophyll concentrations are essential indicators of ocean health and are used to track global phytoplankton levels, crucial for understanding the marine ecosystem. Population: Population data provides insight into the size and growth of human communities, impacting resources, economies, and environmental sustainability. GDP: Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is a key economic indicator that measures the total value of goods and services produced in a country, reflecting economic strength and development
